<details><summary>Abstract</summary>This work aims to adapt a formulation of a geopolymer composite to elaborate complex shapes. Hollow cylinder shape was elaborated from different formulations by casting and additive manufacturing (robocasting or Direct Ink Writing) regarding their feasibility.
